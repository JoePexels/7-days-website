Send Your Booking via WhatsApp First

To complete your reservation, please send your booking details via WhatsApp first. This allows us to:

  • Provide immediate confirmation - Our team responds to WhatsApp messages within minutes
  • Address special requests - We can immediately discuss any specific needs or preferences
  • Confirm room availability - Real-time verification of your selected room type
  • Offer personalized assistance - Direct communication with our reservations team
  • Send confirmation details - We'll provide your booking reference and arrival information
  • Ensure your booking is secured - We hold your room immediately upon receiving your WhatsApp message
  • Quick payment processing - We can send payment instructions directly via WhatsApp

Important: Your booking is not confirmed until you send the details via WhatsApp and receive our confirmation.

Once you've sent your booking via WhatsApp and received our confirmation, return here to click "Complete Booking".
