The Ancient Art of Ethiopian Coffee

At 7Days Motel, we honor the centuries-old tradition of the Ethiopian coffee ceremony, where coffee is more than a beverage—it's a spiritual experience that brings people together.

Our beans are sourced directly from the highlands of Ethiopia, where Arabica coffee originated. We follow the traditional preparation method, roasting green beans over hot coals, grinding them by hand, and brewing in a special clay pot called a jebena.

FAQs

Here are answers to the questions our guests ask most about 7Days Motel Entebbe. If you need anything else, we’re always happy to help.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM, and check-out is until 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request.

Do you offer airport shuttle service?

Yes, we provide complimentary shuttle service to and from Entebbe International Airport for all our guests.

Is parking available at the motel?

Yes, we offer secure on-site parking for all our guests free of charge.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (Ugandan Shillings and US Dollars), major credit cards, and mobile money payments.
